1. Coverage Explanation  
This policy provides financial indemnification and coverage for eligible expenses incurred 
during the policy period, subject to the terms, conditions, and limits specified in the Policy 
Schedule.  
1.1 In -Patient Hospitalization Benefits  
The Company will indemnify the Insured Person for reasonable and customary charges 
incurred for medically necessary treatment during hospitalization exc eeding 24 consecutive 
hours, up to the maximum Sum Insured:  
 Room Rent & Boarding:  Coverage for standard single private room charges, including 
nursing care, RMO charges, and dietary meals provided by the hospital.  
 Intensive Care Unit (ICU):  Actual expenses  incurred for ICU/ICCU admissions without 
sub-limits.  
 Medical Practitioner Fees:  Fees charged by physicians, surgeons, specialists, and 
anesthetists.  
 Procedures & Materials:  Cost of theater charges, anesthesia, blood, oxygen, surgical 
appliances, medicines , drugs, diagnostic tests, and prosthetic devices implanted 
during surgery.  
1.2 Pre - and Post -Hospitalization Expenses  
 Pre-Hospitalization:  Medically necessary expenses incurred up to 30 days  prior to 
the date of admission, provided the condition is direct ly related to the hospitalization 
claim.  
 Post -Hospitalization:  Medically necessary expenses incurred up to 60 days  following 
the date of discharge, directly related to the recovery or follow -up care of the 
condition treated.  
1.3 Daycare Treatments  
Coverage  extends to medical procedures and surgeries that require less than 24 hours of 
hospitalization due to technological advancements (e.g., cataract surgery, dialysis, 
chemotherapy), provided they are performed in a registered daycare center.  
 

2. Exclusions  
The Company shall not be liable to make any payment under this policy for expenses directly 
or indirectly caused by, arising from, or attributable to the following:  
2.1 Permanent Exclusions  
 Cosmetic or Plastic Surgery:  Any treatment, surgery, or procedure undertaken to 
change or enhance physical appearance, unless necessitated by an accident or as 
part of reconstructive surgery following cancer.  
 Rest Cure and Rehabilitation:  Admission primarily for bed rest, custodial care, 
diagnostic evaluation, or rehabil itation that could be managed on an outpatient 
basis.  
 Intentional Self -Injury:  Treatment arising from suicide attempts, self -inflicted 
injuries, or participation in hazardous/adventure sports not explicitly covered by a 
rider.  
 Substance Abuse:  Expenses rel ated to the treatment of alcoholism, drug addiction, 
or abuse of solvent substances.  
2.2 Time -Bound Exclusions & Waiting Periods  
 Initial Waiting Period (30 Days):  Any illness diagnosed or treated within the first 30 
days from the policy inception date, except for claims arising from an unexpected 
accident.  
 Specified Disease Waiting Period (24 Months):  Treatment for specific conditions 
such as cataracts, hernia, joi nt replacements, and benign prostatic hypertrophy are 
excluded for the first two years of continuous coverage.  
 Pre-Existing Diseases (PED):  Any condition, ailment, or injury diagnosed or treated 
within 48 months prior to the inception of this policy will o nly be covered after 36 
months  of continuous policy renewals.  
 
3. Renewal Policy  
To ensure uninterrupted coverage, the policy must be renewed annually before the expiry 
date.

3.1 Grace Period  
A grace period of 30 days  from the date of expiry is allowed for the payment of the renewal 
premium.  
 During this grace period, the policyholder retains continuity benefits (such as waiting 
period credits).  
 Important:  No co verage is provided for any illness contracted or injury sustained 
during the gap between the expiry date and the actual renewal payment date.  
3.2 Cumulative Bonus (No Claims Bonus)  
For every claim -free policy year, the base Sum Insured will be increased by  10%  at the time 
of renewal, up to a maximum accumulation of 50% . If a claim is filed during any policy year, 
the accumulated Cumulative Bonus will be reduced by 10% at the next renewal, but the 
base Sum Insured will never be compromised.  
3.3 Renewal Premium Alterations  
Premiums are subject to change upon renewal based on the age bracket of the insured or 
due to regulatory adjustments approved by the insurance authority. Premium rates cannot 
be altered individually due to an adverse claim history.
