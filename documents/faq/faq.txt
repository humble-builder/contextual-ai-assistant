1. Policy FAQ  
Q: How do I access or download my digital health card?  
A: You can access your digital health card instantly by logging into our customer mobile app 
or the online web portal. Go to My Profile > View Policy Documents > Digital Health Card . 
You can download it as a PDF or save it directly to your smartphone's digit al wallet for easy 
presentation at network hospitals.  
Q: Can I add my spouse or newborn child to my existing policy mid -term?  
A: Spouses can generally only be added during your annual policy renewal period. However, 
a newborn child can be added mid -term wi thin 60 days  of birth by submitting the birth 
certificate and paying a pro -rata premium. Contact our support team directly to initiate a 
mid-term endorsement.  
Q: What happens if I forget to pay my premium on time? Does my policy cancel 
immediately?  
A: No, your policy does not cancel immediately. You are granted a 30-day grace period  from 
your exact expiry date to clear your renewal premium. While your policy remains technically 
active for continuity benefits during this window, please note that no claims wi ll be covered 
for any medical events that occur during the days the policy is un -renewed . 
Q: My base Sum Insured is $50,000. How does the Cumulative Bonus work if I don't file a 
claim?  
A: For every consecutive year you do not file a claim, your Sum Insured  increases by 10%  
($5,000) at no extra cost, up to a maximum cap of 50%  ($25,000). If you have three claim -
free years, your effective coverage becomes $65,000.  
 
 
 
 
 
 

2. KYC Process (Know Your Customer)  
Q: Why is the KYC process mandatory for my insurance policy?  
A: In compliance with national financial and anti -money laundering regulations, insurance 
providers are legally required to verify the identity and address of policyholders. This 
ensures secure financial transactions, prevents identity fraud, and i s strictly required before 
issuing a policy or settling claims above regulatory thresholds.  
Q: What documents are accepted as valid Proof of Identity (POI) and Proof of Address 
(POA)?  
A: We accept high -resolution digital uploads of the following government -issued documents:  
 For Identity:  Passport, Driver's License, National Identity Card, or Voter ID.  
 For Address:  Any of the identity documents listed above (if they display your current 
address), or a utility bill (electricity, water, gas) or bank statement dated within the 
last 3 months . 
Q: How do I complete my KYC verification?  
A: You can complete your verification digitally via our standard or video -KYC paths:  
[Select KYC Link] ──> [Upload Photo ID & Address Proof] ──> [Facial Biometric Match or 
Video Liveness Check] ──> [Instant Approval]  
1. Digital Upload:  During checkout or inside your account dashboard, click Complete 
KYC. Upload clear color photos of the front and back of your selected ID document.  
2. Liveness Check:  The system will prompt you to take a brief smartphone selfie or 
complete a 5 -second video "liveness check" to match your face to your ID photo.  
3. Verification Time:  Digital KYC is automated and typically verified within 5 to 10 
minutes .
